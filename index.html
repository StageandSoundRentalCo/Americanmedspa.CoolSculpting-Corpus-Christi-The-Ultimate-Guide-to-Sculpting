What is CoolSculpting?

CoolSculpting is a non-invasive procedure that uses advanced cryolipolysis technology to target and freeze fat cells. The treatment works by exposing fat cells to controlled cooling, causing them to crystallize and break down over time.

These fat cells are then naturally eliminated by the body, resulting in a slimmer, more sculpted appearance. Unlike traditional weight loss methods, CoolSculpting can target specific areas of the body where stubborn fat often resides, such as the abdomen, thighs, love handles, and double chin.

How Does CoolSculpting Work?

CoolSculpting works by using a process known as cryolipolysis. Here’s a step-by-step breakdown of how it works:

  1. Consultation:
    Your journey starts with a consultation at American Laser Med Spa in Corpus Christi. During this appointment, the team will assess your areas of concern, discuss your aesthetic goals, and determine if you’re a good candidate for CoolSculpting.
  2. Treatment:
    Once your treatment plan is set, the CoolSculpting applicator is applied to the targeted area of your body. The applicator uses a vacuum to pull the tissue into the cooling panels, where the fat cells are exposed to freezing temperatures.
  3. Fat Cell Destruction:
    The cold temperatures cause the fat cells to crystallize and break down. Over time, these fat cells are naturally flushed out of your body through your lymphatic system, resulting in a reduction of fat in the treated area.
  4. Recovery and Results:
    One of the greatest advantages of CoolSculpting is that there is no downtime. After your treatment, you can immediately return to your normal activities. While you may not see instant results, your body will gradually eliminate the treated fat cells over the next few weeks to months.

Most patients begin to notice improvements after about three weeks, with full results visible within two to three months.

What to Expect During and After Your CoolSculpting Treatment?

During the Treatment

CoolSculpting is a non-invasive treatment that is generally well-tolerated by most patients. You may feel a cold sensation during the procedure, but it typically subsides after a few minutes.

Some people experience a mild tingling or pulling sensation as the applicator suctions the skin.

After the Treatment

Since CoolSculpting is non-invasive, there’s little to no recovery time required. You may experience some redness, swelling, or tenderness in the treated area, but these side effects are usually mild and temporary.

You can resume normal activities right away, including work and exercise.

Long-Term Results

CoolSculpting results are not immediate, as it takes time for the body to naturally eliminate the frozen fat cells. Most patients begin to see visible changes in the treated areas within a few weeks, and full results are typically seen after 1-3 months.

The fat cells that are eliminated do not come back, but maintaining a healthy lifestyle can help ensure long-lasting results.
